Cycles

Create meaningful notifications and take control over your schedule.

Your Goals

Sometimes we're forgetful and sometimes we just need a little motivation. Whatever the purpose, Cycles helps you reach those goals with an easy to use interface.

Stand Alone Watch App

Most apps need to be configured on your iPhone. Not Cycles! Any feature can be used directly on your Watch without the interference of another device.

Get Rewarded

Finishing your cycles means you deserve an award. The more cycles you complete, the more trophies you rack up. Who doesn't like trophies, #AmIRight?

Native Interface

There's absolutely no learning curve to using Cycles because everything we designed is meant to be farmiliar to you. Just start up the app on your wrist and tap away!
