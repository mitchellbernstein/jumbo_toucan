Cycles

Create meaningful notifications and take control over your schedule.

Up to 5 Cycles

Need to get more done? You can make up to 5 different cycles, for all your needs! Whether it be to remind you or encourage you.

Customize

You can make cycles for any taks you can imagine. Be alerted to walk the dog, drink a glass of water, or to step away from the computer.

Your Alerts

You have the ability to set when you want to be notified. Up to 2 alerts every hour, but only when you're awake, of course.
